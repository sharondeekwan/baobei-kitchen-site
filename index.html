BaoBei Kitchen

Your personal recipe manager, meal planner, and grocery list.

About

BaoBei Kitchen helps you organize your recipes, plan your meals for the week, and generate grocery lists with one tap. Built for people who love to cook and want to stay organized.

Features

Recipe management with photos, serving scaling, and unit conversion. Meal planning with day and week views. Smart grocery lists with auto-categorization and Apple Reminders export.
