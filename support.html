Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a recipe?

Tap the "+" button on the Recipes tab to create a new recipe. Add a title, ingredients, steps, and photos.

How do I plan my meals?

Go to the Meal Plan tab and tap on any meal slot (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, or Snack) to add a recipe. Use the week view for an overview or day view for detailed planning.

How do I create a grocery list?

You can add ingredients to your grocery list from any recipe (tap the menu button on a recipe) or bulk-add from your meal plan. The app automatically merges duplicate ingredients and organizes them by category.

How do I export my grocery list?

In the Grocery List tab, tap the export button to send your items to Apple Reminders. You can choose an existing Reminders list or create a new one.

Is my data backed up?

Currently, all data is stored locally on your device. We recommend keeping your device backed up via iCloud or your computer. Cloud sync is coming in a future update.
